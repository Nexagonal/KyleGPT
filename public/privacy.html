Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 18, 2026

KyleGPT ("we," "us," "the App") is a satirical messaging application designed to playfully simulate an AI chatbot. In reality, all responses are generated by a human administrator ("Admin"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is handled.

1. Data We Collect

Data Type Purpose Stored Where
Email address Account authentication Firebase Auth + Server DB
Password Authentication Firebase Auth (hashed, never stored by us)
Messages (text & images) Core app functionality; securely delivered to the human Admin Server DB (encrypted)
Device push token Push notifications Server DB
Public encryption key End-to-end encryption Server DB

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV)
  • Browsing or usage analytics
  • Health, financial, or biometric data

3. End-to-End Encryption

All messages are encrypted on your device before being sent to the server using AES-256-GCM encryption with keys derived from Curve25519 ECDH key exchange. This means:

  • The server stores only encrypted ciphertext — it cannot read your messages.
  • Your private encryption key is stored in your device's iOS Keychain and never leaves your device.
  • Only you and the Admin (the intended recipient) can decrypt messages.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Authentication — Your email is used to identify your account and route messages.
  • Messaging — Messages are stored on the server (encrypted) so they can be delivered and retrieved.
  • Push Notifications — Your device token is used to send you notifications when you receive a message. Notification content is generic ("New message") and does not contain message text.

5. AI Training & Model Development

The Admin may use their own response history from conversations to train, fine-tune, or develop language models. Important details:

  • Only the Admin's own messages may be used for this purpose.
  • Your messages will never be used for AI training unless you provide separate, explicit, written consent.
  • Any models developed will not expose your identity or private information.
  • This data is accessed via the Admin's own device (decrypted locally) — the server itself cannot read message content.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose your personal data to any third parties, except:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) — for account authentication only.
  • Apple Push Notification Service — for delivering push notifications.
  • Legal obligations — if required by law, subpoena, or legal process. Note: due to E2EE, we can only provide encrypted data that we cannot decrypt.

7. Data Retention

  • Your data is retained as long as your account exists.
  • Deleted chats are soft-deleted (hidden from your view) but may be retained on the server for administrative purposes.
  • When you delete your account, all associated data (messages, chats, device tokens, encryption keys, Firebase account) is permanently and irreversibly deleted.

8. Data Security

  • All communication uses HTTPS (TLS encryption in transit).
  • Messages are encrypted end-to-end with AES-256-GCM.
  • API access requires Firebase Authentication tokens.
  • Rate limiting and security headers protect against abuse.
  • Encryption keys are stored in the iOS Keychain.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — your messages are visible in the app.
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time.
  • Opt out — you can stop using the service at any time.

10. Children's Privacy

KyleGPT is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data has been collected, it will be deleted immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
